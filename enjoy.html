What I've Been Enjoying

I really enjoy consuming all types of media but I do not always remember everything that ran through my head immediately after watching a incredibly affecting comedy special like Nanette or after listening to a disturbing yet consuming podcast series like Caliphate . Therefore, my intention is to use this page to collect my thoughts from everything I've enjoyed reading, watching, and listening to over the course of the year.
Jan 2019

Get Your New Year's Resolutions to Stick

I began watching Matt D'Avella's videos last year and I've always been impressed by his editing, from audio quality to transitions to music selection, which is always incredibly clean and non-obtrusive to the level that you'd not necessarily even notice it. Something that I liked in particular about this video was Matt's suggestion to construct your dream day and reverse engineer what habits you'd like to form, which I haven't seen in any of the many videos I've seen on this subject.
