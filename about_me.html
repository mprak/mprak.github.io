About Me

My name is Manu Prakasam (MUH-noo PRUH-kah-sum) and I'm a 3rd year EECS student at UC Berkeley. My interests are all over the place but generally I'm passionate about constantly learning more and working to better the world. I eventually hope to work at the intersection of business, technology, design, and social good.

On campus I'm currently president of BEACN , a consulting group focused on environmental and social responsibility.
