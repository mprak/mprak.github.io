Product Candidate Exercise

While recruiting for an APM position, I was invited to complete a product challenge. The exercise was to select a KPI and submit a spec for a new/reimagined feature in the mobile app to improve that KPI . An additional limitation was that the document could be no longer than five slides (not including the title slide) and had to communicate enough information that engineers and designers could get started with the feature. I really had a great time working on this challenge and pushing myself to be creative and a little less pragmatic than usual. I ended up proposing a song of the week feature for the app to introduce users to a different, immersive means of learning (music) and create a differentiated product experience over time to incentivize users to revisit the app regularly (rather than through other strategies like streaks or XP challenges that require a larger level of buy-in from users to care about).
