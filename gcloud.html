Google Cloud Case Competition

In October 2018, I had the great fortune of getting to participate in a Google Cloud Case Competition hosted by DiversaTech with some of my friends from BEACN (Sarah Tang, Grace Pratt, and Kaautam Uthaya Suriyan). The objective of the competition was to make the Google Cloud Platform (GCP) more accessible to students. We ended up coming in 2nd place after coming up with a three-pronged strategy to recognize, understand, and master GCP, leveraging students' familiarity with Google Drive, modifying the GCP website, and utilizing existing on-campus opportunities. This was my first case competition since freshman year (and my second one ever) and it was an absolute blast working and winnning Google Homes with this group!
