My name is Manu Prakasam (MUH-noo PRUH-kah-sum) and I recently graduated with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from UC Berkeley. My interests are all over the place but generally I'm passionate about constantly learning more and working to better the world. I eventually hope to work at the intersection of business, technology, design, and social good.

Last summer, I was a product manager intern at The New York Times , working on the Messaging Capabilities team within the Digital Subscription Growth group. The previous summer, I interned with the Advanced Analytics team at Numerify , an IT analytics startup based in San Jose. At Berkeley, I was president of BEACN , a consulting group focused on environmental and social responsibility.

A few things I love learning or chatting about include, in no particular order: environmental and social responsibility (especially in technology and in fashion), modern world and Latin American history, design and marketing methodologies, data privacy and computer security, the business of entertainment, tennis, and data-driven journalism.
